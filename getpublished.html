Get Published

Times4Everyone is inviting you to submit your best articles about recent topics to possibly be published on the website!


More Details


Rules

  • You must be between ages 12 and 18 when submitting.
  • Entries must be English.
  • Writing must fall under one of the following topics: science, technology, astronomy, biodiversity, the environment, or any other topic that could be published in our specials section.
  • Articles must be written on appropriate topics and recent topics.
  • Entries can include a four-question comprehension quiz at the end. (If you are including a quiz, use examples from the website to get an idea of what we are looking for.)
  • All four quiz questions must include four answer choices.
  • All articles must be original. All photos and information must be cited in MLA8 format. Any form of plagiarism will result in the disqualification of the article.
  • Articles have not been published before.
  • Articles must be longer than 300 words but have no limit.
  • If you have been published on Times4Everyone before, you may submit another article.
  • We are looking for articles of varying difficulty levels from kindergarten to grade 12.



How to Submit

Once you have written your article, submit your writing as a pdf. If you wish to include images, style your article’s format to incorporate them. Include citations at the end of the document. Submit your 4 question exam as a separate pdf in the submission form. Your submission can be edited by Times4Everyone to account for grammatical errors and formatting on the website.
